Many small businesses have unmanaged IT systems and most do not know or recognize the risks they are taking by not managing their IT systems. Symptoms of unmanaged IT system are as varied as the people who operate them. Here are just some of the IT problems we have heard from small business owners with unmanaged computer systems.

Risks of Unmanaged IT Systems & Problems
