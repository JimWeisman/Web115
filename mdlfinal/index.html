About

Introduction

If you want to maximize operational productivity and create competitive marketplace advantages from your IT investment then you will want to begin to know Dave. How to Get There... Let's Get Acquainted. Your business is personal and so is ours. You have questions and so do we. We want to know your problems so we then can go to work and recommend the best solutions for your IT system. Give us a call or contact us and we will arrange a convenient time to talk and meet with you. Let's Get the Facts. Dave will conduct a hands-on inventory and health assessment of your present IT environment. This along with your stated objectives, will be documented as your initial "IT Project Portfolio" - a plan to from where you are to where you want to be. Let's Get Ready. Once you say go, there will be some preparation required for your new IT Department to get "on board". Setting up secure remote access, installing monitoring tools & implementing essential Best Practices. Let's Get Going. We will assume complete responsibility for day-to-day administration, monitoring and maintenance of all your IT infrastructure. You will receive monthly management reports and a face-to-face quarterly review to ensure your IT remains aligned with all your short-term and long-term business goals.
